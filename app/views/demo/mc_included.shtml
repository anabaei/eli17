What is Included?

Included for PC

The Office Professional Plus 2010 suite includes Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, Access, Publisher, OneNote, InfoPath and Microsoft's collaboration tools - Communicator 2007 and SharePoint Workspace 2010.

Microsoft's Live Meeting is already available as a free download and is not included with this suite.

Included for Mac

If your home computer is a Mac you will not be installing Office Professional Plus 2010 suite but Office 2008 instead. What is included with Office 2008 is:


  • Word 2008
  • Excel 2008
  • Powerpoint 2008
  • Entourage 2008
